# Financial Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing financial sales data using Power BI to uncover insights about revenue, profit, product performance, and regional sales trends.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of financial performance across multiple markets and product segments.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations need to understand their financial performance to make informed decisions.

However, raw financial data can be difficult to interpret without proper visualization.

The goal of this project was to create a **financial intelligence dashboard** that provides insights into:

- sales trends
- profitability
- product performance
- regional contributions

---

## Dataset Story

The dataset represents financial sales transactions for a company operating across multiple countries and product categories.

It includes data such as:

- gross sales
- discounts
- profit
- product category
- country
- time period

This allows analysts to evaluate the company's financial health and identify growth opportunities.

---

## Dashboard Objectives

The dashboard answers key financial questions:

- Which products generate the most revenue?
- Which markets are most profitable?
- How do discounts affect profitability?
- What are the sales trends over time?

---

## Dashboard Visualizations

### Revenue Trend (Line Chart)

Displays sales performance over time.

**Why this chart was chosen:**

Line charts are ideal for tracking financial performance and identifying growth or decline patterns.

---

### Sales by Country (Bar Chart)

Compares revenue contributions from different regions.

**Why this chart was chosen:**

Bar charts allow easy comparison between multiple categories.

---

### Profit Distribution (Donut Chart)

Shows how profit is distributed across different segments.

**Why this chart was chosen:**

Donut charts highlight proportional relationships between categories.

---

### Product Performance (Bar Chart)

Highlights top-selling products.

**Why this chart was chosen:**

Businesses need to quickly identify products driving revenue.

---

## Key Insights

Analysis revealed several insights:

1. A small number of products generate the majority of revenue.
2. Some regions outperform others in terms of sales performance.
3. Discount strategies significantly affect profit margins.
4. Revenue trends show seasonal variations.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the findings:

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing products.
- Reevaluate discount strategies to protect profit margins.
- Expand operations in high-performing markets.
- Monitor seasonal sales trends for better forecasting.
