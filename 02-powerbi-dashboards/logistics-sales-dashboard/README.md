# Logistics & Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project involved building an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze logistics operations and sales performance for MEL LLC.

The objective was to transform raw operational data into meaningful insights that could support better business decisions regarding shipment efficiency, sales performance, and regional distribution.

This project was completed as part of the **Blossom Academy Data Analytics Fellowship training program**.

---

## Business Problem

Logistics and sales operations generate large amounts of data. However, without proper visualization and analysis, it becomes difficult for management to answer key questions such as:

- Which regions generate the most revenue?
- Which products perform best?
- Who are the top-performing salespeople?
- Are shipments being delivered efficiently?

The goal of this dashboard was to provide **a clear, visual overview of operational performance**.

---

## Dataset Story

The dataset represents the operations of a logistics company managing:

- product shipments
- regional sales
- delivery performance
- salesperson activity

The data contains information such as:

- shipment status
- product types
- revenue generated
- country distribution
- sales representative performance

This data allows the company to monitor **both logistics efficiency and revenue performance**.

---

## Dashboard Objectives

The dashboard was designed to answer the following business questions:

1. How much revenue is being generated?
2. Which countries generate the most sales?
3. Which products contribute the most revenue?
4. Who are the top-performing sales representatives?
5. What is the status of shipments?

---

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes the following visuals:

### KPI Cards
Used to display key metrics such as:

- Total Revenue
- Total Shipments
- Total Profit
- Active Sales Representatives

These provide **quick high-level insights for decision makers**.

---

### Revenue Trend Chart (Line Chart)

This chart shows revenue over time.

**Why this chart was used:**

Line charts are ideal for showing **trends and patterns over time**.

This helps identify:

- seasonal patterns
- growth trends
- potential revenue declines

---

### Revenue by Country (Bar Chart)

This chart compares revenue across different countries.

**Why this chart was used:**

Bar charts are effective for comparing categories and quickly identifying:

- top markets
- underperforming regions

---

### Product Performance (Bar Chart)

This chart highlights which products generate the highest sales.

**Why this chart was used:**

Businesses need to identify their **best-performing products** to prioritize production and marketing efforts.

---

### Shipment Status Distribution (Donut Chart)

This chart shows the proportion of shipments that are:

- delivered
- pending
- delayed

**Why this chart was used:**

Donut charts effectively show **proportional distribution of categories**.

---

## Key Insights

From the dashboard analysis, several insights were identified:

1. A few regions contribute a significant portion of total revenue.
2. Certain products drive the majority of sales.
3. Some shipments experience delays which may affect customer satisfaction.
4. A small number of sales representatives contribute a large share of revenue.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the insights:

- Increase focus on high-performing regions.
- Improve operational efficiency to reduce delayed shipments.
- Provide incentives for high-performing sales representatives.
- Investigate underperforming markets to identify growth opportunities.
