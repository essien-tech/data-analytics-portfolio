# Projects

## 1. Logistics & Sales Performance Dashboard

**Tool:** Power BI

This project analyzes logistics and sales data to provide insights into shipment performance, product revenue distribution, and regional sales trends.

The dashboard helps answer key business questions such as:

* Which regions generate the most revenue?
* Which products drive sales performance?
* What is the distribution of shipment statuses?
* Which sales representatives contribute most to revenue?

**Key Features**

* KPI performance indicators
* Revenue trend analysis
* Product performance comparison
* Shipment status distribution
* Regional sales analysis

**Insights**

* A small number of regions contribute the majority of revenue
* Certain products dominate sales performance
* Shipment delays may impact operational efficiency

---

## 2. Financial Performance Dashboard

**Tool:** Power BI

This dashboard focuses on financial sales data, providing insights into revenue trends, profit distribution, product performance, and regional market performance.

The objective was to transform raw financial data into a **business intelligence dashboard that supports strategic decision-making**.

**Key Features**

* Sales trend analysis
* Profitability breakdown
* Product performance insights
* Market comparison across regions

**Insights**

* A few products drive most of the company's revenue
* Some regions significantly outperform others
* Discount strategies impact overall profitability

Across these projects, I apply a structured analytical workflow:

3. **Exploratory Analysis**

4. **Visualization & Dashboard Development**
